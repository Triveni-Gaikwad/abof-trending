EVENT REVIEW- IIFA 2015

97 LIKES

“ Facing a slew of deadlines and the gloom that so often accompanies summer's end, I was suddenly desperate to escape the demands of daily life. „

I have no idea what prompted the unorthodox request. Surely, there was something delicious about knowing I wouldn’t have to get ready for bed that evening? No matter what the motive, this much I do clearly recall: I revelled in every second of that day. “To hell with social protocol!” I might have thought, as I sashayed down the Trent-Severn Waterway’s weather-beaten docks. This was living!  

Admittedly, my night-time wardrobe has undergone something of a sartorial devolution in the years since I first pioneered innerwear as outerwear. Back-to-school shopping, I suppose, prioritised classroom-appropriate outfits over those worn to bed. In place of snuggly flannel nightgowns or polka-dotted PJs, I soon found myself slipping into threadbare souvenir tees and faux-varsity sweatpants and, of course, anything I could successfully steal from my brother’s dresser. If this haphazard accumulation was somehow lacking – in what, exactly? – I failed to notice.

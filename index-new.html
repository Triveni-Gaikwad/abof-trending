LOOK OF THE DAY

6th May, 15

BEST LOOKS FROM PARIS

97 LIKES Hello Guys! Today I'm thrilled to show you my latest collaboration with the very well kown French luxury shoe maker house Berluti who recently came up with its own Prêt-à-porter line created for men with impressive sense of style and aesthetic. Mixing an olive green double breasted blazer with a total white look, I finished my outfit with some brown leather accessories. I hope you guys like this shoot and I will see you very soon for more. Not only I was lucky to dig for their nicest pieces in their amazing Flagship store on 120 rue du Faubourg Saint Honoré in Paris but I also got the opportunity to shoot them in a very privileged spot : Le Louvre.
